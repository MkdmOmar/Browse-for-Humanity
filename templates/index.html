How Does It Work?

We crowdsource processing power from thousands of our volunteers to help solve your complex problems with the power of parallel processing

Why Should You Volunteer?

Just by downloading our extension and letting it run in the background, you help researchers and scientists solve large complex issues. In return for lending your processing power, we take our revenue and directly donate it to the refugee crisis. So you browse the web to help humanity
